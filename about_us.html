Cyberguard is a news company dedicated to informing and educating the public about cybercrimes, digital security, and sustainable technology practices. Our goal is to raise awareness of digital threats while fostering a security culture that respects the environment.

At Cyberguard, we believe digital protection should go hand in hand with sustainability, promoting practices that reduce the environmental impact of digital operations and encouraging responsible use of resources. We work to provide accurate information and practical guidance, helping companies and individuals protect themselves from cyber threats while adopting more sustainable solutions.
